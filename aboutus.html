About Us

Our core foundation has been to innovate and do more than just create.

We are more than a real estate company. Our hybrid business model allows us to function as a combination of a real estate development firm, a think tank of IT solutions and innovative cloud kitchen setups aka. Ghost Kitchens. In short, we aim to address the gap between real estate, architecture, IT and engineering.

In an age where new business models are emerging every day, backed by the latest technologies, we have come to understand that survival alone is not an option. Moreover, to thrive in this competitive environment, innovative solutions are the only way to move forward. The ideas we implement in our projects are not just lifestyle driven, they also ensure environmental sustenance.

Several ideals ignite our passions and keep us focussed on what matters. These core values are the building blocks of who we are and what we stand for.

Our Founders

Mrs. Dhrutii Sankpal, Vice-Chairman.

A Master Architect and Interior Designer by profession, she is the creative driving force of the group. Her creative expertise has always added strategic value to all our projects.

People
Driving a passion for design and compassion for our communities.
We have a self-motivated management team in place with ample years of experience in the reality sector. They ensure that every project built not just meets and maintains the quality and design standards but is also in par with international standards.

Vision

We create spaces with meaning.

We envision to not just develop spaces but also understand the need to cherish the environment, take only what is needed and preserve the rest for coming generations.

Our aim is to be a realty major that plays a vital role in providing unparalleled living experience and create neo-modern living spaces.
