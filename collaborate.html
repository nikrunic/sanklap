96 Monsoon Villas, Lonavala


Tucked away from the chaos of the city lies our flagship project, 96 Monsoon Villas, nestled amongst the scenic range of Sahyadri at Lonavala. Surrounded by green foliage, the villa stands as an epitome of futuristic architecture with remarkable designs and extravagance at its best. The villa has been built using innovative materials, incorporating luxurious amenities and environment-friendly solutions that represents a revolutionary ‘green’ concept.

Balador, Talegaon


This 45-acre township is a work of art, inside and out. Balador is our first attempt at creating smart townships which is a perfect combination of location, architecture and urbanism. At 2000 feet above sea level, overlooking the Sahyadri mountain range, the township gives its residents a multitudinous array of both residential and commercial spaces with luxurious amenities, acoustic comfort and a tranquil environment.
